Financial Freedom

Financial Freedom falls under the Heritage Foundation's category of "Market Openness", which also includes "Trade Freedom" and "Investment Freedom". An accessible and efficiently functioning formal financial system ensures the availability of diversified savings, credit, payment, and investment services to individuals and businesses. According to the Heritage Foundation, the useful role for government here lies in ensuring transparency and integrity and promoting disclosure of assets, liabilities, and risks. Regulations that go beyond the assurance of transparency and honesty in financial markets can impede efficiency, increase the costs of financing entrepreneurial activity, and limit competition.

This graph shows a strong association between Financial Freedom and Overall Economic Freedom. The pairwise correlation is 0.79.
