Regions

This visualization shows the average overall economic score by region. The Hertiage Foundation considers above 80 to be "economically free", and none of the regions meet that criteria as a group. The best scoring region is Europe, where the average score for the countries in that region is 69. The highest scoring country in Europe is Switzerland, with an 82, and the lowest score is Ukraine with a 53. The poorest performing region is Sub-Saharan Africa, where the average is 54.
